Welcome to Lamoni

Graceland University

Graceland University is a private four-year institution that sits in the middle of the town. The estimated enrollment for the school is around 1,000. There are many fields of study offered.

Linden Street Coffee House

Looking for a relaxing envrionment to sit down and a cup o' joe or two? Look no further, the Linden Street Coffee House has the perfect atmosphere and great customer service. Stop by any time within the business hours!

The Coliseum

The Coliseum is Lamoni's own little movie theatre here in town. It opens weekends and has a select movie playing. The Coliseum also holds Thursday night movies for the students of the university.

Livestock Auction

The place to get your livestock in Lamoni. Its located just east of HyVee and they hold auctions weekly.

Lamoni Community Center

The Lamoni Community Center is a place for people of the local community to gather and have a good time.

My Experience in Lamoni

Living in Lamoni has been a different experience since i am from Arizona, but I've come to have an appreciation for the small-town lifestyle. Lamoni is a very small town and doesn't provide you with much to do or many food options. This place has a way of bringing people together. While living here I have grown as an individual and progressed in my life's journey. I'm proud to say I will be graduating April 29th and have already accepted a position at a nearby organization.
